CAD models are the digital recreation of of your idea necessary for proof of concept and 3D printing. When your project becomes a CAD model, you can see it three dimensionally and easily make quick, clean adjustments to it. If you want to have your model 3D printed, it is super easy because the file is already formatted to be sent to a 3D printer. If you have your idea sketched out and want it printed, have one of our CAD experts make it into a model for you.

3D printing is a production process that creates items from a digital (CAD) model. The benefit of this process is that it significantly cuts the cost for prototyping and low volume production. Rather than having to get a manufacturer to create a unique process to produce your idea, have one of our 3D printer makers produce it quickly and affordably!
