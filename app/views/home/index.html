Who we are

It starts with who we were: A couple of guys with some 3D printers. Like many makers, we were frustrated by how hard it was to find customers, and customers would tell us how frustrating it was to find a broad selection of serious makers. Having experienced these problems first hand, we knew creating Zertica was the obvious solution.
