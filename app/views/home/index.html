Super simple and safe. When you submit your project to be bid on, we will ask for your credit card information. Our secure system will hold the bid amount in an escrow account until your project is completed. At that point, we will then charge the card. Your information is never passed on to the maker.

We are confident in our system and our process but we are also realistic that at some point, sometime, somewhere, something will not go as planned. Our answer to that is simple: money back guarantee. We will also want to follow up with you to find out more about the experience so that we can learn and improve.

Pretty easily actually. One of the most frustrating things we encountered when we were 3D printing was how time consuming and frustrating it was to keep our customers informed of our progress. To fix that, we have integrated a messaging system for you and the maker to communicate as well as a status section on your order page where you can track the order!

At the same time a customer submits a projects, we also ask for their credit card information. After the project has been delivered, we charge the credit card and deliver the funds to your account. If something is wrong with the credit card, that is our problem… you will be paid!

We actively get involved. Fortunately, our site is built so that the entire process is documented and clearly outlined. We will go back through the order history to understand what happened and make things right. If the dispute is murky, we will make everyone whole. However, for both customers and makers, we monitor quality because we want only quality people interacting on our site. We will not hesitate to ask someone to leave if we do not like his or her conduct.

The makers are responsible for shipping. While you can choose to pass it on or at least part of it to the customers, it is up to you to include that amount in your bid. You will have enough information to be able to evaluate how much it will cost to ship. Of course, if you are doing CAD work, there are no shipping fees.

CAD drawings is the digital model of your idea! When your project become a CAD model, you can see it three dimensionally and easily make quick, clean adjustments to it. If you want to have your model 3D printed, it is super easy because the file is already formatted to be sent to a 3D printer. If you have your idea sketched out and want it printed, have one of our CAD experts make it into a model for you since it needs to be in that file to be printed.

3D printing is a production process that creates items from a digital (CAD) model. The benefit of this process is that it significantly cuts the cost for prototyping and one off modeling. Rather than having to get a manufacturer to create a unique process to produce your idea, have one of our 3D printer makers produce it quickly and affordably!
